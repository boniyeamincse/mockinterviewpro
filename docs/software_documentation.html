📋 Overview

The Mock Interview Platform is a comprehensive virtual training solution designed to help students build confidence and improve their interview skills through realistic practice sessions with experienced trainers.

This platform enables trainers to monetize their expertise while providing students with accessible, affordable interview preparation.

Platform Objectives

  • Empower students to practice interviews in a safe, interactive environment
  • Enable trainers to reach a wider audience and generate income
  • Create a seamless user experience with intuitive booking and payment systems
  • Foster a supportive learning community through reviews and feedback

🚀 Core Features

📅 Event Creation

Trainers create interview events with customizable session structures, topics, and pricing (e.g., 12 sessions × 30 minutes for 1000 BDT)

🔍 Browse & Discovery

Students browse different interview categories, filter by type and trainer rating, and view detailed session information

📱 Session Booking

One-click session booking with automatic calendar integration, confirmations, reminders, and rescheduling options

🎥 Video Interface

Real-time HD video streaming with trainer video on right, student on left, screen sharing, and optional recording

💳 Payment System

Secure payments with automatic 15% commission: Student pays 100 BDT → Trainer gets 85 BDT, Platform gets 15 BDT

⭐ Reviews & Ratings

Students rate trainers (1-5 stars) and submit detailed feedback; trainers can respond and track ratings trends

👥 User Types and Roles

Trainers

Role: Experienced professionals who conduct interview sessions and earn through the platform.

Trainer Capabilities

  • Create and manage interview events
  • Conduct mock interview sessions via video
  • Track income and view balance management
  • View and respond to student reviews
  • Analyze student feedback and performance data
  • Schedule and manage all upcoming sessions

Students (Users)

Role: Individuals preparing for interviews seeking practice and professional guidance.

Student Capabilities

  • Browse and filter available trainers and events
  • View trainer profiles, ratings, and reviews
  • Book sessions and manage bookings
  • Make secure payments through the platform
  • Attend live video sessions with trainers
  • Submit reviews and ratings after sessions
  • Track session history and learning progress

💰 Payment System

Commission Structure

The platform uses a transparent 15% commission model:

Amount Trainer Receives Platform Receives Student Pays
100 BDT (single session) 85 BDT (85%) 15 BDT (15%) 100 BDT
1000 BDT (package) 850 BDT (85%) 150 BDT (15%) 1000 BDT
500 BDT (half package) 425 BDT (85%) 75 BDT (15%) 500 BDT

Payment Formula

Trainer Amount = Total Amount × 0.85

Platform Amount = Total Amount × 0.15

Payment Features

  • ✅ Multiple payment methods (Credit/Debit cards, mobile banking, digital wallets)
  • ✅ Secure, encrypted transactions (PCI DSS compliant)
  • ✅ Complete transaction history for both parties
  • ✅ Automated invoice generation for tax purposes
  • ✅ Real-time balance updates for trainers
  • ✅ Flexible refund and cancellation policies
  • ✅ Withdrawal system for trainer earnings

📅 Session Management

Session Lifecycle

  1. Creation: Trainer creates event with multiple sessions, published to platform
  2. Booking: Student books session, payment processed, confirmation sent
  3. Pre-Session: Reminders sent, join link activated 1 hour before
  4. Active: Video feeds initialize, interview conducted following structure
  5. Post-Session: Recording stored, review prompt sent, feedback notifications

⚙️ Technical Specifications

System Requirements

Client Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Minimum 2 Mbps internet speed for HD video
  • Camera and microphone (for video sessions)
  • JavaScript enabled

Server Requirements

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • RESTful API architecture
  • WebRTC for real-time video streaming
  • Database for user and transaction management
  • Message queue for notifications
